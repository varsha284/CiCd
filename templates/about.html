About Campus Lost & Found

The Campus Lost & Found Portal is designed to help students, faculty, and staff of our college easily report and recover lost items. Our mission is to create a safe and efficient system for reuniting people with their belongings.

How It Works

  1. Report Lost Items: Students can report lost items with detailed descriptions, photos, and contact information.
  2. Report Found Items: Anyone who finds an item can report it to help the owner recover it.
  3. Search & Match: Our search system allows users to find items based on various criteria.
  4. Claim Process: Users can claim ownership of items, and admins verify claims before returning items.

Features

  • User-friendly web interface
  • Secure authentication system
  • Image upload for item identification
  • Advanced search functionality
  • Admin dashboard for management
  • Mobile-responsive design
